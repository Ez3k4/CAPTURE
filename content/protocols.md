## Liposomes {#h3}

{{< protocol name="Polyvinyl alcohol 5 % preparation">}}
{{% protocol-content %}}

**Aim:**

Protocol for dissolving Polyvinyl alcohol (PVA) in water.

**Materials:**

- PVA, fully hydrolyzed

- Milli-Q water

- Magnetic stirrer

**Procedure:**

1. Add Milli-Q water to PVA at an end concentration of 5 % (w/v).

2. Using a magnetic stirrer, heat up the solution to 90 Â°C for 4-6 h until the all of the PVA is dissolved.
